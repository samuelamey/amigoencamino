PROPERTY INFORMATION

Hostal Ville Camille is a modern lodge located in San José de la Montaña just a 15,4 Kms from Internacional Airoport Juan Santamaría on highways 123 and 114, heading from downtown of Barva de Heredia, Costa Rica, Centroamérica.

You can get to the town of San José de la Montaña using a low-cost public buses from the city of Heredia or from the center of the city of Barva.

We offer confortable rustic indoors for any kind of travellers - families with/out children, pairs, yuongsters or mayor persons for short or long stay.

You`ll be proveid with Wi-Fi and Internet conexion all over the place.

Bedcloth, towels and hot water in a shower are included as well as the laundry and a bicicle is available to ride sightseeng the nearabouts of the lounging. You are invited to free walks exploring the Porrosatí River, Sacramento Chapel, Roblealto Camping and pueblito`s downtown.



The willing traveller will be assessed for the visit of the Braulio Carrillo Nacional Park or for the Canopy adventure (diligency or orientation is according to the purpose of your interest). Maps, touristic information and a handy advice for better use of your time and money in our country you`ll obtain in our website: Amigo en Camino

The reception is open 24 hours and there is no restriction on time of check in/chek out. The tenants may store their luggage (cost-off ) even after leaving if/when they wish to visit other places of the country.

Ours is a tiny town borded by tropical woods and a virgen Nature, its falks are gentle and goodhumored; we have supermarkets, cafeterias, fast food establisments and other services.

Dare to visit us and we will surely make your adventure pleasant and hospitable. It`s a truely lovely place…

No age restrictions - we are glad to people from 0 to 100. You are welcomed!

Politics of Payment In The Hostal

You may book your space by phone call or on line: www. amigo en camino.com and you will pay personally on your arrival in a “cash-on a nail” manner. We accept US dollars, Euros or colons (local device) only. The money exchange is based on official refference of the Central Bank of Costa Rica. The price does not include other charges. By any means, if you couldn`t/didn`t pay on a due time you will be charged with the first night rate. Please, be sure to make us know previously if you arrive later than it was planned.
